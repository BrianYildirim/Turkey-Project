Economy


Turkey's top exports are gold, cars, delivery trucks, vehicle parts, and jewellery. Turkey exported $139B worth of exports. Turkey is the 25th largest export economy in the world.
Some natural materials and resources used were coal, iron, ore, copper, chromium, atimony, murcury, gold, limeston, clay, and many more.
